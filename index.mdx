## Summary
- New to Aseed? Read [What is Aseed](/what-is-aseed) first.
- Want a fast result? Follow [Quick Start: First Project](/quickstart).
- The workflow moves from project setup to reports and sharing.
- Each step below links to a dedicated guide.

## The workflow at a glance
1. Create a project and describe your research. [Create a project](/projects/create)
2. Add research context with goals and an interview guide. [Add research context](/projects/context)
3. Upload interview files. [Upload interviews](/interviews/upload)
4. Transcribe and review transcripts. [Transcription](/interviews/transcribe) and [Edit transcripts](/interviews/edit)
5. Generate single reports for each interview. [Single reports](/reports/single)
6. Build a project report to synthesize insights. [Project report](/reports/project)
7. Share or export the final report. [Share publicly](/sharing/public) or [Export](/sharing/export)
