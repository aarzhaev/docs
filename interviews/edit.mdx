## Overview
Clean transcripts lead to better insights and fewer report revisions.

## Step-by-step guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a transcript">
    Select an interview from Project Records to view its transcript.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review accuracy">
    Scan for misspellings, speaker mistakes, and missing context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make edits">
    Correct errors and save the updated transcript.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices
<Tip>
Fix speaker names early so reports read clearly.
</Tip>
