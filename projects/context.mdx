## Overview
Research context tells Aseed what you are trying to learn and how the interviews are structured.

## Step-by-step guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Research Context">
    Inside your project, go to the **Research Context** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add research goals">
    Describe the questions you want to answer or hypotheses you want to test.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the interview guide">
    Paste your interview script or topic list so Aseed understands the flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices
<Tip>
Write goals as outcomes, for example "Understand why users abandon onboarding."
</Tip>
