## Overview
A project is the container for your research. It groups interviews, context, and reports so you can synthesize results in one place.

## Step-by-step guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new project">
    In your workspace, click **Create Project**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name and describe the project">
    Add a clear project name and a short description of the research goal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add files now or later">
    You can upload interviews during creation or in Project Records after the project is created.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices
<Tip>
Use a naming pattern like "Product - Study - Month" so projects are easy to scan.
</Tip>
