## Overview
Custom reports let you define the exact sections and questions you want Aseed to answer.

## Step-by-step guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Custom tab">
    In the report setup, switch to **Custom**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add sections and prompts">
    Choose system sections or write your own prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order and save">
    Drag to reorder sections, then save the structure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices
<Tip>
Keep custom prompts specific and tied to your research goals.
</Tip>
