## Overview
Editing reports helps you improve clarity, correct issues, and align output with stakeholder expectations.

## Step-by-step guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the report">
    Select the single report or project report you want to edit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make updates">
    Adjust content or structure based on your review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Regenerate if needed">
    Regenerate the report to reflect the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices
<Tip>
Update research context or transcripts before regenerating for better results.
</Tip>
