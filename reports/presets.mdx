## Overview
Presets are ready-made report structures that help you generate consistent results quickly.

## Step-by-step guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the report setup">
    In the project report flow, open the **Presets** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a preset">
    Choose the template that matches your research goal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and generate">
    Save the structure and generate the report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices
<Tip>
Use presets when you want speed and consistency across projects.
</Tip>
