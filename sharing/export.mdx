## Overview
Exporting lets you share reports in formats suitable for presentations and documentation.

## Step-by-step guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the report">
    Select the report you want to export.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose export">
    Click **Export** and pick the format you need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the file">
    Save the export locally or share it with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices
<Tip>
Export after final edits so the shared version stays consistent.
</Tip>
