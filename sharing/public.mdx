## Overview
Public links make it easy to share reports with people who do not have Aseed access.

## Step-by-step guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the report">
    Choose the report you want to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable public sharing">
    Turn on the public link and review access settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the link">
    Share the link with stakeholders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices
<Tip>
Include a short summary when you send the link so readers know what to focus on.
</Tip>
